Our Founder

Mr Billu

Meet Mr. Billu, the world’s most self-assured feline. He insists on watching you in the bathroom like he's your personal bodyguard, and every time you open a book, he sits directly on the page—because clearly, he's more interesting than whatever you're reading. His favorite hobby? Chasing invisible bugs with Olympic-level intensity, followed by dramatic flops on the floor like he's fainted from exhaustion. He also has a weird obsession with sitting inside the refrigerator every time it’s open, as if he’s contemplating becoming a leftover himself.
